Designer

As a member of the club Design for America (DFA) at UW. I designed a project called Deskmates. Deskmates is has an interactive screen and mailboxes designed to help students with ADHD/ADD excel and focus better in a classroom.

Leader

For one of my Interactive Media Design classes, I took on the role of leader for my team and together we designed a game called NECO.NECO focuses on raising awareness about climate change and mental health issues in society. It takes a satirical view of the impending doom of the world but also provides a platform for individuals to come together and discuss these issues.

Activist

As the Creative Design Director for the UW club Tech++, I have learned how to empower others to work together, regardless of their major, and use technology to bridge the gap between technology and other industries such as business, art, and even fashion (image is from Tech++'s 2018 fashion show). Currently, I am planning and designing a more improved fashion show for 2020.
